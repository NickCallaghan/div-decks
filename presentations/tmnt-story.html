The Origin

A canister of mutagen changes everything

New York City

The Accident

  • A young boy carries four pet turtles across a New York street
  • A truck swerves to avoid him, sending a canister of radioactive ooze flying
  • The canister shatters in the sewer, drenching the turtles in glowing mutagen
  • The ooze triiiggers an extraordinary transformation

The Sensei

Master Splinter

  • Hamato Yoshi, a skilled martial artist, flees Japan after a bitter rivalry with Oroku Saki
  • Living in the sewers of New York, he finds the four mutated turtles
  • Exposed to the same ooze, Yoshi transforms into a humanoid rat
  • He names the turtles after his favourite Renaissance artists and trains them in ninjutsu

The Turtles

Four brothers, four personalities, four weapons

Meet the Brothers

Leonardo
Twin Katana
The disciplined leader. Leo is devoted to his training and takes his responsibility as the eldest seriously. He wears a blue mask and looks to Splinter for guidance above all else.
Raphael
Twin Sai
The hothead. Raph is strong, aggressive, and fiercely loyal. He wears a red mask and frequently clashes with Leo over leadership, but his passion drives the team forward in battle.
Donatello
Bō Staff
The brains. Donnie is the inventor, engineer, and tech expert. He wears a purple mask and would rather solve problems with science than fists — though his bō staff says otherwise.
Michelangelo
Nunchaku
The party dude. Mikey is the youngest, the funniest, and the most free-spirited. He wears an orange mask and keeps morale high with his love of pizza and relentless optimism.

The Villains

The Foot Clan and its ruthless leader

Antagonist

The Shredder

  • Oroku Saki, Splinter's bitter rival from Japan
  • Leader of the Foot Clan, a deadly ninja army
  • Wears razor-sharp armour from head to toe
  • Obsessed with destroying Splinter and his students

Allies

Friends on the Surface

  • April O'Neil — fearless journalist and first human ally
  • Casey Jones — hockey-mask-wearing vigilante
  • The turtles live in secret but protect the city above
  • A hidden family fighting for an unaware world

The Enemy

The Foot Clan

  • An ancient ninja clan with roots in feudal Japan
  • Hundreds of faceless soldiers trained in stealth and combat
  • Operations span organised crime, technology theft, and world domination
  • The turtles' most persistent and dangerous enemy

The Legacy

From indie comic to global phenomenon

Cultural Impact

A Franchise Like No Other

  • Created by Kevin Eastman and Peter Laird as a black-and-white indie comic in 1984
  • Spawned multiple animated TV series, each redefining the turtles for a new generation
  • Six feature films spanning live-action and animation
  • Billions in merchandise, video games, and a permanent place in pop culture
